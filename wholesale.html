What We Do

High-Volume Supply You Can Count On

RJ Produce is a full-service wholesale distributor of fresh fruits and vegetables, supplying retailers, foodservice operators, and regional distributors across the U.S. and Mexico. Operating from our Pharr, Texas facility — minutes from the U.S.–Mexico border — we move high volumes of produce quickly and efficiently, maintaining cold-chain integrity from the moment product leaves the field.

Our buying power, border-region location, and long-standing grower relationships allow us to offer competitive pricing on a consistent, year-round supply — even on seasonal and specialty items that are harder to source elsewhere.
