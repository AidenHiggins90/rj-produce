What we do

High-volume supply you can count on

RJ Produce is a full-service wholesale distributor of fresh fruits and vegetables, supplying retailers, foodservice operators, and regional distributors across the U.S. and Mexico. Operating from our Pharr, Texas facility — minutes from the border — we move high volumes quickly and efficiently, maintaining cold-chain integrity from the moment product leaves the field.

Our buying power, border-region location, and long-standing grower relationships let us offer competitive pricing on a consistent, year-round supply — even on seasonal and specialty items that are harder to source elsewhere.

What's included

Full-service distribution

Most customers come to us tired of juggling separate suppliers, schedules, and paperwork. We fold all of it into one relationship: a single point of contact who knows your account, deliveries timed to your receiving dock rather than our convenience, and a check on every load before it leaves the building so nothing shows up that you'd have to turn away. Reorders, standing orders, and your full purchase history live in one online dashboard, so a buyer can run the whole account in the few minutes between everything else on their plate.
