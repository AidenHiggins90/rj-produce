Who We Are

A Trusted Name in Produce Since 2004

Founded by Rick Gorena in Pharr, Texas, RJ Produce was built on a simple promise: deliver the freshest, highest-quality produce while maintaining the integrity and relationships that make great business possible.

Over two decades, we've grown into a trusted wholesale partner for major national retailers — supplying branded product to Costco, Restaurant Depot, and beyond. Our deep roots in both domestic and Mexican agriculture mean year-round availability and unmatched sourcing flexibility.

Where We Source

Rooted in Two Countries

RJ Produce sources directly from growing regions across the United States and Mexico. Our border-region location puts us at the intersection of two of the world's most productive agricultural nations, giving our partners year-round access to a broader product range than either country alone could support.

By working directly with farms on both sides of the border — no brokers, no middlemen — we deliver better pricing, tighter quality control, and greater sourcing flexibility for every account we serve.
