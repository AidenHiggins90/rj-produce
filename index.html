What we do

One partner across the entire produce supply chain

From the field to your dock, RJ Produce owns every link — so you get fresher product, fewer hand-offs, and a single team accountable for the result.

Direct Sourcing

Contracted relationships with growers across Texas, the Southwest, and Mexico give us first access to seasonal and specialty produce — no brokers in between.

Logistics & Cold Chain

Our Pharr, Texas hub sits minutes from the busiest produce border crossing in North America — keeping transit short and the cold chain unbroken from field to shelf.

Supply-Chain Technology

Powered by WholesaleWare, buyers get real-time inventory visibility, streamlined digital ordering, and transaction history — the whole account managed online.

Sourcing network

Rooted in two of the world's most productive growing regions

Positioned at the U.S.–Mexico border, RJ Produce taps both markets at once — domestic reliability and the depth, variety, and value of Mexican agriculture — to keep partners supplied no matter the season.
