Sustainability

Responsible Sourcing, Every Season

We believe great produce and responsible practices go hand in hand.

Seasonal Sourcing

We align sourcing with natural growing cycles, reducing the need for forced production and supporting healthier farming practices across our grower network.

Local Grower Support

By sourcing directly, we invest back into farming communities on both sides of the border, building long-term economic relationships that benefit everyone in the chain.

Waste Reduction

Our efficient supply chain and direct grower model minimizes handling steps, reducing spoilage and food waste across the distribution network.

Our Commitment

Building for the Long Term

Sustainability at RJ Produce isn't a marketing campaign — it's how we've operated since 2004. Our direct grower model means shorter supply chains, less fuel, less packaging, and less waste. When we build relationships with farms, we build them to last.

As a GrubMarket company, we continue to invest in technology and processes that make fresh produce distribution more efficient and more responsible for everyone in the chain.
