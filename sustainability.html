Sustainability

Responsible sourcing, every season

We believe great produce and responsible practices go hand in hand.

Our approach

Working with nature, not against it

For us, responsible practice is mostly a matter of not fighting nature. We buy what's ready when it's ready, following each region's natural growing calendar instead of pushing farms to force a crop out of season. Because we deal with growers directly, the money stays in the farming communities on both sides of the border, season after season, rather than thinning out across a chain of intermediaries. And the shorter that chain runs, the less produce is handled, jostled, and left waiting — which is the simplest way we know to keep good food from ending up as waste.

Our commitment

Building for the long term

Sustainability at RJ Produce isn't a marketing campaign — it's how we've operated since 2004. Our direct grower model means shorter supply chains, less fuel, less packaging, and less waste. When we build relationships with farms, we build them to last.

As a GrubMarket company, we continue to invest in technology and processes that make fresh produce distribution more efficient and more responsible for everyone in the chain.
