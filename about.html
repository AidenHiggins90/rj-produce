Our Story

From a Single Truck to a Trusted Name

In 2004, Rick Gorena started RJ Produce out of Pharr, Texas with little more than a handful of grower relationships and a belief that the produce business runs on trust. In a region where most operators chased the lowest price, he bet on something different: showing up, keeping his word, and treating both growers and buyers as long-term partners.

Those early relationships became the foundation. As farmers on both sides of the border learned they could count on RJ Produce season after season, the business grew — from local deliveries to supplying branded product to some of the largest retailers in the country, including Costco and Restaurant Depot.

In 2021, that two-decade track record drew the attention of GrubMarket, and RJ Produce joined their family of companies. The partnership brought new technology and national reach, but the way we do business hasn't changed since day one — the same handshake, the same standards, the same people.

Our Values

What Drives Us Every Day

The values that started this company still run it. We keep the commitments we make, even when they cost us something — in this business a name is earned one honored handshake at a time, and lost just as quickly. We belong to the Rio Grande Valley and stay invested in the growers and families on either side of the border who make the work possible. And we hold ourselves to a level of discipline — in our facilities, our processes, and our scheduling — that lets a partner expect the exact same RJ Produce on a quiet Tuesday as on the busiest week of the year.
