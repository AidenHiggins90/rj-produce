Our Process

From Field to Your Door

A simple, reliable supply chain built on decades of grower trust and operational excellence.

01

Source

We work directly with growers in Texas and Mexico to select the best seasonal produce at peak freshness.

02

Quality Check

Every shipment passes through our Primus Labs certified facility with full food safety inspection and quality assurance protocols.

03

Deliver

Fast, reliable distribution to retailers and foodservice operators across the U.S. with consistent scheduling you can count on.

Why It Matters

Fewer Hands, Fresher Produce

Every step we remove from the supply chain means less time in transit, less handling, and fresher produce on your shelves. By owning the sourcing relationship and operating our own certified facility, we cut out the inefficiencies that plague traditional distribution.

Our digital order management system — powered by WholesaleWare — keeps you informed at every stage, with real-time inventory visibility and streamlined ordering.
