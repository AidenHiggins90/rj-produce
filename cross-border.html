Cross-Border Sourcing

Direct access to Mexico's growing regions — fresh produce with fewer steps and faster delivery.

Our Advantage

A Border-Region Edge No One Else Has

Pharr, Texas is one of the busiest produce crossing points in North America. RJ Produce sits minutes from that border — which means we can source directly from Mexican growers, clear customs faster than any distributor operating from the interior, and put fresher product in front of U.S. buyers at prices that reflect the savings.

We've built trusted, long-term relationships with farms in Sonora, Sinaloa, and the Rio Grande Valley — relationships that give us early access to seasonal harvests, preferred pricing, and the flexibility to accommodate custom volume requests that most distributors can't fill.

How It Works

From Mexican Farm to U.S. Buyer

Direct Grower Relationships

We contract directly with farms in key Mexican growing regions, eliminating broker markups and giving us consistent access to quality product throughout the season.

Border-Ready Logistics

Our Pharr location means faster customs clearance, shorter transit times, and reduced cold-chain exposure compared to distributors routing product through distant inland hubs.

Compliance & Food Safety

All cross-border shipments meet USDA, FDA, and FSMA import requirements. Our Primus Labs certification and internal protocols ensure every load clears customs clean.

Seasonal Specialty Access

Mexico's diverse growing regions produce items unavailable or limited in the U.S. market. Our grower network gives buyers access to specialty and off-season varieties year-round.
