Cross-Border Sourcing

Direct access to Mexico's growing regions — fresh produce with fewer steps and faster delivery.

Our Advantage

A Border-Region Edge No One Else Has

Pharr, Texas is one of the busiest produce crossing points in North America. RJ Produce sits minutes from that border — which means we can source directly from Mexican growers, clear customs faster than any distributor operating from the interior, and put fresher product in front of U.S. buyers at prices that reflect the savings.

We've built trusted, long-term relationships with farms in Sonora, Sinaloa, and the Rio Grande Valley — relationships that give us early access to seasonal harvests, preferred pricing, and the flexibility to accommodate custom volume requests that most distributors can't fill.

How It Works

From Mexican Farm to U.S. Buyer

The stretch between a Mexican field and a U.S. dock is where most produce loses its edge — and where most distributors lose control of it. We stay hands-on the whole way. Harvest volume is committed before the season opens, so supply is locked in rather than chased on the spot market when prices spike. Crossings are run by people who clear this border every day and know how to keep a load moving through inspection instead of baking on the tarmac. What lands on your dock is the cold chain unbroken — and a range of varieties and harvest windows the domestic market alone can't put in front of you.
