Wholesale Distribution

High-volume supply you can count on

RJ Produce is a full-service wholesale distributor of fresh fruits and vegetables, supplying retailers, foodservice operators, and regional distributors across the U.S. and Mexico. Operating from our Pharr, Texas facility — minutes from the border — we move high volumes quickly, maintaining cold-chain integrity from the moment product leaves the field.

Most customers come to us tired of juggling separate suppliers and schedules. We fold it into one relationship: a single point of contact, deliveries timed to your dock, a check on every load before it ships, and reorders plus full purchase history in one online dashboard.

Cross-Border Sourcing

A border-region edge no one else has

Pharr, Texas is one of the busiest produce crossing points in North America. We source directly from Mexican growers, clear customs faster than any distributor operating from the interior, and put fresher product in front of U.S. buyers at prices that reflect the savings.

Harvest volume is committed before the season opens, so supply is locked in rather than chased on the spot market. Crossings are run by people who clear this border every day — what lands on your dock is the cold chain unbroken, plus varieties the domestic market alone can't offer.

Private Label & Branded

Shelf-ready products under your brand

RJ Produce operates a full private label and branded program for national retailers and regional chains, reaching major accounts including Costco and Restaurant Depot under both the RJ Produce brand and custom retailer labels.

A private label program only works when it disappears into your operation. We build each one to the retailer's exact requirements — pack size, PLU codes, the nutritional panel — and run it through the audited food-safety process buyers in this category insist on. One account manager owns it from first proof to ongoing reorders.
