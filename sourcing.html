Sourced from the United States and Mexico

Direct grower access on both sides of the U.S.–Mexico border, delivering year-round freshness.

The best of both countries

Rooted in two of the world's most productive regions

RJ Produce sources directly from growing regions across the United States and Mexico. Our border-region location in Pharr, Texas gives us a natural advantage — positioned at the intersection of two of the world's most productive agricultural nations, we tap both markets at once and move product faster than any distributor operating from a single country.

U.S. farms provide reliable domestic supply for buyers who require it. Mexican agriculture brings an unmatched depth of seasonal variety, volume, and value. Together, they give our partners year-round access to a broader product range than either country alone could support.

Why our location matters

Geography does a lot of the work

Produce picked in northern Mexico can reach our Pharr facility the same day it crosses, and be on a customer's truck before a distributor sourcing from the coasts has even cleared inspection — and every hour saved early shows up as extra shelf life at the far end. Time isn't the only payoff. Twenty years rooted in one spot have earned us a place at the front of the line when a grower's supply runs short — the kind of standing that can't be bought, only built season after season.
