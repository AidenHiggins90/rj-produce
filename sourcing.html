Our Sourcing Network

The Best of Both Countries

RJ Produce sources directly from growing regions across the United States and Mexico. Our border-region location in Pharr, Texas gives us a natural advantage — we're positioned at the intersection of two of the world's most productive agricultural nations, allowing us to tap both markets simultaneously and move product faster than any distributor operating from a single country.

U.S. farms provide reliable domestic supply for buyers who require it. Mexican agriculture brings an unmatched depth of seasonal variety, volume, and competitive pricing. Together, they give our partners year-round access to a broader product range than either country alone could support.

Why Our Location Matters

Gateway Location

Pharr, TX sits at one of the busiest commercial border crossings in the U.S., giving us unmatched logistics speed from Mexican farms.

Faster to Market

Shorter transit times from both domestic and Mexican farms means produce arrives fresher with a longer remaining shelf life.

Grower Trust

Two decades of relationships with farming families on both sides of the border gives us preferred access during peak demand periods.

Competitive Pricing

Direct sourcing without broker markups, combined with volume relationships, translates to better pricing passed on to our partners.
