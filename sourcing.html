U.S. & Mexico Sourcing

Rooted in two of the world's most productive regions

RJ Produce sources directly from growing regions across the United States and Mexico. Our border-region location in Pharr, Texas puts us at the intersection of two of the world's most productive agricultural nations — we tap both markets at once and move product faster than any distributor operating from a single country.

U.S. farms provide reliable domestic supply; Mexican agriculture brings an unmatched depth of seasonal variety, volume, and value. Together they give partners year-round access to a broader product range than either country alone could support. Produce picked in northern Mexico can reach our facility the same day it crosses — and every hour saved early shows up as extra shelf life at the far end.

Sustainability

Working with nature, not against it

For us, responsible practice is mostly a matter of not fighting nature. We buy what's ready when it's ready, following each region's natural growing calendar instead of pushing farms to force a crop out of season. Because we deal with growers directly, the money stays in the farming communities on both sides of the border, rather than thinning out across a chain of intermediaries. And the shorter that chain runs, the less produce is handled, jostled, and left waiting — the simplest way we know to keep good food from ending up as waste. Sustainability at RJ Produce isn't a campaign; it's how we've operated since 2004.
