U.S. & Mexico Sourcing

Rooted in two of the world's most productive regions

RJ Produce sources directly from growing regions across the United States and Mexico. Our border-region location in Pharr, Texas puts us at the intersection of two of the world's most productive agricultural nations — we tap both markets at once and move product faster than any distributor operating from a single country.

U.S. farms provide reliable domestic supply; Mexican agriculture brings an unmatched depth of seasonal variety, volume, and value. Together they give partners year-round access to a broader product range than either country alone could support. Produce picked in northern Mexico can reach our facility the same day it crosses — every hour saved adds shelf life on the other end.

Sustainability

Working with nature, not against it

We follow each region's natural growing calendar rather than push farms to force a crop out of season. Because we buy directly from growers, more of what you pay stays in farming communities on both sides of the border instead of going to intermediaries. And fewer hand-offs means less handling and less waste. Sustainability at RJ Produce isn't a campaign — it's how we've operated since 2004.
