Management strategy & capital allocation

How we grow — and how we deploy capital

Our priorities are simple: compound the platform, integrate disciplined acquisitions, and stay profitable while we do it.

Invest in AI & the platform

The largest share of capital goes into GrubMarket AI and unifying the portfolio under GrubOS — the highest-return use of our data advantage.

Disciplined, accretive M&A

We acquire strong software and distribution businesses at sensible multiples, then integrate each into an existing module — compounding, not diluting.

Profitable, self-funding growth

Three years of EBITDA profitability and a strong balance sheet mean we grow on our own cash flow — not at the mercy of capital markets.
