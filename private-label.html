Services

Private Label & Branded

Your brand on our product — retail-ready packaging backed by certified quality and consistent supply.

What We Offer

Shelf-Ready Products Under Your Brand

RJ Produce operates a full private label and branded produce program for national retailers and regional chains. Our products reach major accounts including Costco and Restaurant Depot under both the RJ Produce brand and custom retailer labels — packaged, labeled, and inspected to meet the strictest retail requirements.

We handle sourcing, packing, labeling, and delivery, so your team receives a finished, shelf-ready product. Volume commitments are flexible, and our border-region operation means faster replenishment cycles than you'd get from distributors sourcing exclusively from California or Florida.

Built for Retail Standards

Custom Packaging

We pack to your specs — bag size, label design, PLU codes, nutritional requirements. Retail-ready product arrives on your floor without extra handling at your end.

Primus Labs Certified

Every private label shipment is packed in our Primus Labs certified facility. Full food safety documentation is available on request for compliance with your supplier requirements.

Dedicated Account Support

Private label customers get a dedicated account manager who coordinates sourcing, monitors harvest timelines, and keeps your program running without surprises.

Consistent Year-Round Supply

Cross-border sourcing and multi-region grower relationships keep your program stocked through seasonal transitions. We manage supply continuity so you don't have to.
