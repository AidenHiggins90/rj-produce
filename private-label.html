Private Label & Branded

What we offer

Shelf-ready products under your brand

RJ Produce operates a full private label and branded produce program for national retailers and regional chains. Our products reach major accounts including Costco and Restaurant Depot under both the RJ Produce brand and custom retailer labels — packaged, labeled, and inspected to meet the strictest retail requirements.

We handle sourcing, packing, labeling, and delivery, so your team receives a finished, shelf-ready product. Volume commitments are flexible, and our border-region operation means faster replenishment cycles than distributors sourcing exclusively from California or Florida.

Program details

Built for retail standards

A private label program only works when it disappears into your operation — your name on the bag, your specs on the label, and none of the headaches on your side of the dock. We build each one to the retailer's exact requirements, down to pack size, PLU codes, and the nutritional panel, then run it through the audited food-safety process buyers in this category insist on, documentation included. One account manager owns the program from first proof to ongoing reorders, watching grower timelines so a seasonal handoff never becomes an empty shelf wearing your brand.
